Leg Cast Prototype

Leg Cast Demo


Purpose

Develop an orthopaedic cast workflow

Process

  • 3D scan and process point cloud into a mesh
  • Using mesh/medical software such as mercuris or meshmixer, adjust the mesh and move the respective keypoints into their correct position and divide the body part into different segments
  • Load the divided into the Hyperganic core to add various features such as embossing images/words, perforated holes and automatic placement of components onto the casts
